## 2. Offer framing & CTA copy

**Question:** Should the offer be headlined as **"£50 off"** or **"£10 off × 5 orders"**?

**Current assumption (brief):** Lead with the £50 hook — *"Get £50 Off When You Switch to Fresh"* / CTA: *"Claim My £50 Saving"* — with the £10×5 breakdown as supporting detail in the offer box.

**Sheet wording:** *"£10 off each of your first 5 orders"* / CTA: *"Shop Fresh"* / *"Claim £10 off x5"*.

**Why it matters:** The £50 number is a stronger hook but implies a single upfront discount; the £10×5 is more accurate to how the offer works. Whichever is confirmed drives hero callout, offer-box heading, CTA button copy, and any ad creative.
