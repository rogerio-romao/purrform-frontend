About Purrform

Purrform was born out of one simple belief: cats are obligate carnivores who thrive on fresh, minimally processed food — and they deserve nothing less.

Founded by pet nutrition enthusiasts and cat lovers, Purrform set out to close the gap between what cats need and what most commercial cat foods actually deliver. After years of research, working alongside veterinary nutritionists, and testing with cats who had a very strong opinion on the matter, Purrform Fresh Deli was created.

We believe in transparency, quality, and keeping things simple. No sugar. No filler. Just fresh, wholesome food your cat will go absolutely wild for.

What Makes Purrform Fresh Different

High Meat Content, Always

Every ingredient in Purrform Fresh Deli meets human food-grade standards. We use the same quality cuts of meat and produce you’d find in a quality butcher - not the by-products and rendered offcuts common in mass-market pet food.

The Health Benefits of Fresh Cat Food

Switching to fresh food isn’t just a treat for your cat, it’s a powerful step towards better long-term health and happiness. Here's what cat owners notice when they make the switch:

Better Digestion

Fresh, minimally processed food is far easier for cats to digest than heavily processed kibble or tinned food. Fewer digestive upsets, less bloating, and firmer stools - your cat's gut will thank you.

A Shinier, Healthier Coat

The natural fats and proteins in Purrform Fresh Deli nourish your cat's skin and coat from the inside out. Many owners report a noticeably softer, shinier coat within just a few weeks.

Healthy Weight Management

Without the unnecessary carbohydrates found in many commercial cat foods, Purrform Fresh Deli supports a healthy metabolism and helps cats maintain an ideal body weight - reducing the risk of obesity-related conditions.

Improved Hydration

Cats evolved to get most of their moisture from food, not just a water bowl. Fresh food has a naturally high moisture content, supporting kidney and urinary tract health - a common concern in cats.

More Energy & Vitality

A diet rich in high-quality protein fuels lean muscle mass and sustained energy. Owners consistently report their cats seem livelier, more playful, and more engaged after making the switch.

Stronger Immune System

Fresh food retains natural vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that are often destroyed in high-heat processing. This helps support your cat's immune system and overall health.
