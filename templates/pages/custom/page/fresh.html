About Purrform

Purrform was born out of one simple belief: cats are obligate carnivores who thrive on fresh, minimally processed food — and they deserve nothing less.

Founded by pet nutritionists and lifelong cat lovers, Purrform set out to close the gap between what cats need and what most commercial cat foods actually deliver. After years of research, working alongside veterinary nutritionists, and testing with cats who had a very strong opinion on the matter, Purrform Fresh Deli was created.

We believe in transparency, quality, and keeping things simple. No sugar. No filler. Just fresh, wholesome food your cat will go absolutely wild for.

What Makes Purrform Fresh Different

Human-Grade Ingredients, Always

Every ingredient in Purrform Fresh Deli meets human food grade standards. We use the same quality cuts of meat and produce you'd find in a quality butcher — not the by-products and rendered offcuts common in mass-market pet food.

Made Fresh, Delivered to Your Door

We prepare every meal by hand and gently freeze it to lock in natural goodness — keeping it fresh from our kitchen to your door. No preservatives, no factory cooking. Just fresh food, every time, made for your cat.

Vet-Nutritionist Approved Recipes

Our recipes have been developed and approved in collaboration with qualified veterinary nutritionists to ensure every meal is complete, balanced, and optimised for feline health at every life stage.
