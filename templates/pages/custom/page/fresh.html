The Benefits Cat Owners Notice

Switching to fresh food isn't just a treat for your cat — it's a powerful step towards better long-term health and happiness. Here's what cat owners notice when they make the switch:

Better Digestion

Fresh, minimally processed food is far easier for cats to digest than heavily processed kibble or tinned food. Fewer digestive upsets, less bloating, and firmer stools - your cat's gut will thank you.

Shinier Coat

The natural fats and proteins in Purrform Fresh Deli nourish your cat's skin and coat from the inside out. Many owners report a noticeably softer, shinier coat within just a few weeks.

More Vitality

A diet rich in high-quality protein fuels lean muscle mass and sustained energy. Owners consistently report their cats seem livelier, more playful, and more engaged after making the switch.

Complete Nutrition

Balanced recipes designed to support lifelong feline health. Without the unnecessary carbohydrates found in many commercial cat foods, Purrform Fresh Deli supports a healthy metabolism and ideal body weight.

Improved Hydration

Cats evolved to get most of their moisture from food, not just a water bowl. Fresh food has a naturally high moisture content, supporting kidney and urinary tract health — a common concern in cats.

Stronger Immune System

Fresh food retains natural vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that are often destroyed in high-heat processing. This helps support your cat's immune system and overall health.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Purrform Fresh Deli?

Purrform FRESH DELI is a complete fresh cat food, gently cooked, and made with natural ingredients with high meat content. No artificial flavourings or preservatives added.

What makes Purrform Fresh different?

Freshly prepared meals made with human-grade ingredients, grain-free, no fillers or added preservatives or sugar, made in the UK, and approved by veterinary nutritionists.

Is it complete and balanced?

Yes, every recipe is formulated to provide complete nutrition for cats at all life stages.

How is it delivered?

Meals are frozen immediately after preparation and delivered directly to your door.

Can I mix Fresh with my current food?

Yes. We recommend transitioning gradually over 7–10 days to allow your cat's digestive system to adjust.

How do I defrost or store Fresh Deli?

All our cat food is delivered frozen and should be stored in your freezer until you are ready to use it. Defrost in your refrigerator overnight — it will last 3–4 days in the fridge once defrosted. If you won't use a full tub, allow it to slightly defrost until soft enough to portion, then refreeze the remainder to avoid waste.

How much do I feed my cat?

For a 3.5kg cat, we recommend feeding 2 pouches a day. Use our online calculator to work out the right daily amount for your cat: www.purrform.co.uk/rawsome-catculator/

Is this suitable for kittens?

Yes. Fresh Deli is formulated for all life stages, including kittens.

Is this suitable for cats with allergies or sensitivities?

This depends on the specific ingredient your cat is allergic or sensitive to. If your cat is allergic to a particular protein, such as chicken or beef, any food containing that protein may trigger a reaction. For cats with food sensitivities, we generally recommend introducing a recipe made with a novel protein source.
